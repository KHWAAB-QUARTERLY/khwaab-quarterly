I Strive

\ \
\ I strive to be loved, to be truly seen,\ yet that has always been the hardest part.\ \ I feel unlovable; I am a beautiful kind of chaos,\ too much, too deep, too difficult to hold.\ Before the person I love,\ I erase myself without hesitation.\ \ I place them above me,\ above my needs, my pride, my quiet dignity.\ My self-respect and ego dissolve in their presence,\ fading like shadows at sunrise.\ \ I long to feel their heartbeat quicken\ when I do something unexpected,\ to watch them grow nervous around me,\ unsure of where to place their hands or their thoughts.\ \ All I want is to feel their eyes linger on me,\ as if, for a moment,\ no other girl in this world exists.\ \ I want them to drown in the depth of me,\ to ache for me, to burn softly with my absence,\ to miss me in ordinary places,\ and hold me close like something they are afraid to lose.\ \ I want to matter so deeply\ that the thought of losing me\ would leave tears where words could not.\
